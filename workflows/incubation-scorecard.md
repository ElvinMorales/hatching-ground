# Incubation scorecard

Score each criterion from 1 (poor) to 5 (strong). Scores support judgment; they do not cancel safety or hatch-gate failures.

| Criterion | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Personal usefulness | Rare or speculative value | Occasional real value | Solves frequent, meaningful friction |
| MVP clarity | Outcome is undefined | Main loop is partly clear | One input, output, user, and test are explicit |
| Build simplicity | New platform/infrastructure | Several artifacts or one integration | Few files and a simple reviewed workflow |
| Privacy safety | High exposure or consequential action | Sensitive data can be contained | Synthetic/non-sensitive or safely local data |
| Reuse potential | One-off with no reusable pattern | Some reusable artifacts | Reusable pattern without broadening MVP |
| Maintenance lightness | Continuous/high-touch upkeep | Periodic manual upkeep | Static or easily refreshed artifacts |
| Fun/momentum | Strong resistance | Neutral interest | Exciting enough to finish and use |

## Recommendations

- **Hatch now** — all hatch gates are clear and the MVP is worth testing.
- **Hatch after one more clarification** — one material, answerable gap remains.
- **Park for later** — useful, but timing or prerequisites are poor.
- **Split into smaller ideas** — multiple independent loops obscure the MVP.
- **Merge with another idea** — it duplicates or completes an existing candidate.
- **Discard for now** — weak value, unacceptable risk, or no credible test.

## Synthetic example

| Idea | Usefulness | Clarity | Simplicity | Privacy | Reuse | Maintenance | Fun | Total | Recommendation |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Garden task note formatter | 4 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 3 | 5 | 4 | 31 | Hatch now |
| Universal household operator | 3 | 1 | 1 | 2 | 4 | 1 | 3 | 15 | Split into smaller ideas |

Record one-sentence evidence for every score and note uncertainty. Compare totals only after applying privacy and hatch gates.
